About OSK

Born from
Consensus

OSK — short for Oskar — is the core token of the Pangu Community. The name mirrors the Oscar Awards, reflecting its founding vision: decentralized finance for the global film and entertainment industry.

"A once-in-a-lifetime DeFi & NFT project powered by a self-governed community of 100,000+ members."

Founded on October 22, 2020 in China by six millennial DeFi enthusiasts, Pangu grew entirely through community consensus — no venture capital, no corporate backing. OSK was mined exclusively by community members staking FIST tokens into smart contracts on the TRON blockchain.

Today, with 94.16% of supply burned or locked, OSK is among the most deflationary tokens on TRON — traded live on SunSwap V2.
